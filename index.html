How We Work

Process.

From start to finish, we strive to provide our clients with an industrious and professional service. It's what we love to do!

1.

Initial Enquiry

Client brief providing site details and any other relevant information to formulate a site-specific quotation.

2.

Flight Planning

We carry out a desktop study and prepare detailed RAMS, including prior notifications and airspace permissions.

3.

Site Inspection

We attend site and undertake the flight in compliance with on-site health & safety requirements.

4.

Data Delivery

The data capture is quality assured and packaged for delivery within 24hrs of inspection.

Understanding

The Benefits.

UAVs are fast replacing more conventional methods of visual inspection, offering our clients with a wide range of benefits.

Reduced Risk

Safety

Drones improve worker safety on site by avoiding dangerous situations.

Cost Savings

Financial

Drones save time and money, compared to scaffolding or hiring a cherry picker.

Quality Data

Inspection

Drones collect accurate data to create a detailed record of an asset's lifecycle.

Efficiency Gains

Workflow

Drone inspections vastly reduce the amount of time it takes to inspect an asset.
