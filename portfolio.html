How We Work

Process.

From start to finish, we strive to provide our clients with an industrious and professional service. It's what we love to do!

1.

Initial Enquiry

The client provides a brief, site details and any other relevant information to formulate a site-specific quotation.

2.

Flight Planning

We carry out a desktop study and prepare detailed RAMS, including prior notifications and airspace permissions.

3.

Site Inspection

We attend site and carry out our inspection in compliance with on-site health & safety requirements.

4.

Data Delivery

The data capture is quality assured and packaged for delivery within 24hrs of inspection.
